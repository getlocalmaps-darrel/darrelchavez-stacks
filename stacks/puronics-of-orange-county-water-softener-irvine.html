Frequently Asked Questions — water softener Irvine

Q: Do I need a water softener in Irvine, CA?

A: Yes — Orange County water averages 15-25 grains per gallon of hardness. A water softener eliminates scale buildup, dry skin, spotty dishes, and appliance damage caused by hard water.

Q: What is SilverShield HYgene technology?

A: SilverShield HYgene is NASA-developed silver ion technology that inhibits bacterial growth inside water filter media. It's the same technology used on the Space Shuttle and by the U.S. Army.

Q: Do you offer free water testing in Orange County?

A: Yes. Puronics of Orange County offers free, no-obligation in-home water testing throughout Irvine and Orange County. Call (714) 545-3022 to schedule.

Q: What certifications do Puronics systems have?

A: Puronics systems are NSF/ANSI 44 certified for water softening, NSF/ANSI 42 certified for aesthetic effects, and carry the WQA Gold Seal. The company has been BBB Accredited since 1991.

Q: Is there a salt-free option for water conditioning?

A: Yes. Puronics offers the Clarius and Goodspring no-salt filtration lines that condition water without salt, potassium, or chemicals. These are ideal for homeowners who prefer an environmentally friendly option.
